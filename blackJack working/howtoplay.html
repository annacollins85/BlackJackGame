How to play

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand. Both the player and the dealer start with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end. To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stick' is to hold your total and end your turn. The dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher. If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand. If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10), you got a blackjack.
